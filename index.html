Who is Pantum ?

Pantum Electronicsis responsible for the business operation of Pantum printers, specializing in planning, R&D, production, sales, marketing and service of Pantum branded laser pritners. Its business covers printers, genuine consumables and related printing solution and service.

Pantum Electronicsis the subsidiary for global sales of Pantum printers except China. Its sales network has covered all over the world. There are branch companies in Europe and USA. The Pantum authorized service and technical support centers locate in main markets of the world, building up an extensive service network.

After Apex acquiring Lexmark, Pantum International will leverage advantages of our brother company to further enlarge Pantum product line and better serve the global users.
